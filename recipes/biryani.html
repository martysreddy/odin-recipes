Description

Biryani is a popular South Asian rice dish known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices.
It typically consists of long-grain Basmati rice cooked with a variety of ingredients such as chicken, lamb, beef, or vegetables.
The dish is layered, with the rice and meat or vegetables cooked separately before being combined and further cooked together.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken, lamb, beef, or vegetables
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Bay leaves
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cloves
  • Coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • Mint leaves
  • Saffron
  • Warm milk
  • Ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • Salt
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Nuts and raisins (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Cook meat or vegetables with spices and set aside.
  3. Saute onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  4. Layer rice and meat/vegetable mixture in a pot.
  5. Sprinkle with saffron-infused milk and ghee.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until flavors meld.
  7. Garnish with fried onions, nuts, and herbs before serving.
