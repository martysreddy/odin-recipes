Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish known for its rich, creamy tomato-based sauce and tender chunks of marinated chicken.
It's mildly spiced and bursting with flavor, often served with rice or naan bread for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cream
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, garam masala, chili powder)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Cilantro (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, and spices in a bowl.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in butter until soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Add cream, simmer for 5 minutes, then adjust seasoning.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
